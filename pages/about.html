Our Story

Born from a
Love of Skin.

Amare was born in 2023 from a simple frustration: effective skincare was either out of reach or filled with ingredients that didn't belong on skin.

Founded by Sophia Reyes, a beauty industry veteran with over a decade of formulation experience, Amare set out to prove that you don't need a luxury price tag to get luxury results. Every product in our range is formulated with clinically proven actives, tested by dermatologists, and crafted with full ingredient transparency.

Amare means "to love" in Latin and Italian — because we believe that skincare is the most intimate form of self-care, and every product should feel like love applied to skin.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To make science-backed skincare accessible to every Filipino — crafted with transparency, formulated with integrity, and delivered with genuine care.
