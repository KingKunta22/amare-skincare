About Amare

Amare was born in 2020 from founder Maria Lopez's belief that skincare should be an act of self-love. Frustrated with harsh chemicals and empty promises, she created a line that combines nature's best ingredients with modern science.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted skincare brand in the Philippines, known for transparency, sustainability, and effectiveness.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, natural skincare solutions that empower everyone to feel confident in their own skin. We are committed to cruelty-free practices, eco-friendly packaging, and community education.
